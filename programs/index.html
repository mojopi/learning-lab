To support young people in figuring out who they are and what they want to do, staff act as supportive mentors and introduce them to communities in which they can explore your interests and develop their skills. Young people join in and find what resonates with them so that they can branch out and do their own projects. Staff's projects help to give young people exposure to different communities, ideas, and tools. As young peole branch out and do their own projects, they develop their creative capacities, technical, and project management skills.
