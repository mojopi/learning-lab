We want to create a learning lab seeks to design, create, and document new kinds of immersive educational experiences, which support high school students and recent graduates in leveraging their tastes and interests to do projects that develop their creative capacities and technical expertise. We aspire to be a new kind of educational organization that supports young people, especially those facing structural oppression, in doing real creative work that reflects their passions and which sets them up for success in their next chapter.
